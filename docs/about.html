Hi, I’m Rolando

I am a product designer. My skills and experience span across the entire design process. Broadly speaking, this includes research, interaction design and visual design. It all combines to create elegant, intelligent solutions.

I had a mini-career as a fashion model spanning 6 years with a few breaks to try other things. I worked in a few cities and countries. My most high-profile jobs were GQ and Men’s Health.

This was my first professional project. It’s a football social betting app for Rio de Janeiro based startup, Pitaco, that lived in the App Store for a year. I worked with my design classmate, Pedro do Ó.

Now, I’m enjoying life in Sweden. It’s such a nice, clean place and the tech talent is incredible. When I’m not designing, I’m either cruising around Stockholm or trying not to get beat up in Muay Thai.
